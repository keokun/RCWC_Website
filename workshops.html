PAST WORKSHOPS


December 6th
Game Knight! Story cubes were used to create a story as a group. We also played Pictionary.


November 29th
Each person was given a photo of a location and had time to write a description without using words that would give away the location. Each person then read their description to the group and everyone had to guess the location.


November 15th
Everyone sat in a circle and each started writing a story. After several minutes, the stories were passed to the next person, who continued the story. The time each person had to write decreased each time they were passed until everyone had their original papers back. The stories were then read aloud to the group.


November 7th
Each person wrote a story using only dialogue.


November 1st
Everyone selected a stock photo of a person and a prompt. Each person wrote about their character. Then, in groups of three, the characters were combined into a single story based off of the prompts.


October 24th
Everyone was given a Halloween-themed prompt at random and had twenty minutes to come up with a short story. Afterwards, we discussed tips for National Novel Writing Month preparation and set word count goals.


October 11th
Everyone was given a different stock photo of a person. In ten minutes, each person created a character based off the stock image, defining personality, career, motivations, family, lifestyle, etc. Afterwards, everyone was separated into groups of three or four people. Each group was given three words at random. The goal was to combine each group member's character and each of the three words into a cohesive story plot.
